AI and Law Seminar 2024

Organized by the AI Institute, DS-KTLab, University of Engineering and Technology, VNU-HN and the Center for Juris-Informatics, Japan.

Overview

This seminar will explore key topics such as knowledge graphs, logic in legal AI, large language models, named entity recognition, and information retrieval. Attendees will have the opportunity to learn from leading experts in the field and explore collaboration opportunities in the application of AI in the legal domain. For more information, visit our announcement on the AI Institute's Facebook page.

Date and Time: 18:00 - 19:00, November 5, 2024

Venue: Room 212, Building E3, University of Engineering and Technology, VNU-HN

Agenda

  • 18:00 - 18:05 | Opening Remarks and Program Introduction
  • 18:05 - 18:15 | Presentation on Knowledge Graphs - Kristiina Jokinen, AI Research Center, AIST Tokyo Waterfront
  • 18:15 - 18:25 | Presentation on Logic in Legal AI - Wachara Fungwacharakorn, Center for Juris-Informatics
  • 18:25 - 18:35 | Presentation on Large Language Models - Nguyen Ha Thanh, Center for Juris-Informatics & Center for LLM
  • 18:35 - 18:45 | Presentation on Named Entity Recognition - May Myo Zin, Center for Juris-Informatics
  • 18:45 - 18:55 | Presentation on Information Retrieval and Query Expansion - Vuong Thi Hai Yen & Nguyen Hai Long, DS-KTLab, UET-VNU
  • 18:55 - 19:00 | Closing Remarks
